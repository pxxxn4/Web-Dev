Recipe #1 - Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: Pancakes
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 250 ml
Egg 1 pc
Flour 200 g
Sugar 2 tbsp
Baking powder 1 tsp
Oil 1 tbsp

Step 1: Mix egg, milk and sugar.

Step 2: Add flour and baking powder.

Step 3: Stir until smooth.

Step 4: Fry pancakes on a hot pan until golden.
